ABOUT COMPANY

Transforming Automotive Care, One Detail at a Time

We’re here to make your car look better than new. Since 2016, we’ve been the go-to car detailing hub across India, offering services like ceramic coating, PPF, car washes, and steam detailing. We use only the best products in the industry—like IGL Coatings, the most sold eco-friendly coating brand worldwide. And here’s the exciting part—we’ve embraced a franchise model, so you can join us in revolutionizing the automotive care industry.

Active Detailing is dedicated to elevating car care standards by providing unparalleled detailing services. Our mission is to enhance and protect the beauty of vehicles through cutting-edge techniques like ceramic coating and Paint Protection Film. We aim to empower franchisees to deliver exceptional automotive aesthetics and customer satisfaction.

Active Detailing envisions revolutionising the car care industry by consistently delivering unparalleled quality and innovation. Committed to exceeding customer expectations, we strive to be the global leader in automotive detailing, setting new standards for excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, while fostering a network of successful franchise partners.
